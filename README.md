# Human Squares

**Draw what you feel. On a square. The world responds.**

Human Squares is a global art experiment where anyone can express raw, unfiltered creativity inside a single square — then share it, see it analyzed by AI, and let the community vote it into the canon.

We ship the kit at cost: a thick stack of premium square pads and a Sharpie. You bring the soul. The platform does the rest.

- 🟦 **The Pad** — Heavyweight square paper, designed for ink. Shipped at cost.
- 🖊️ **The Sharpie** — One pen. One color. Infinite ideas.
- 📱 **The Platform** — Upload, analyze, vote, follow, collect.
- 🧠 **The AI** — Tells you what your square *might* mean. Compares. Curates. Surprises.
- 🗳️ **The Crowd** — Up votes. Down votes. Curated sets. Featured artists.
